About Mylo

Welcome to Mylo, a modern legal management platform designed to help you track and organize your legal cases, meetings, and client information.

Our platform allows lawyers and legal teams to efficiently manage client data, track case progress, and ensure timely communication with clients.

Whether you're a solo practitioner or part of a larger law firm, Mylo is the tool you need to stay organized and focused on what matters most: your clients and their cases.

The content on this platform will evolve as you input case and client data. Stay tuned for more features as we continue to improve the application!

Security & Flexibility

Mylo is a secure SaaS application built with privacy in mind. All your data is encrypted and company-specific — meaning your information is fully isolated and accessible only to your organization.

There is no need to download or install anything. Being fully cloud-based, it eliminates risks such as viruses, software conflicts, or outdated versions.

Looking for a custom screen or report? Simply send us a message via the Feedback section, and we'll design and integrate it directly into your system — with no technical effort required on your side.

We believe that software should adapt to your workflow — not the other way around. Mylo is fast, clean, and continuously evolving based on your needs.

Supported Devices

You can use our system on computers and tablets with full compatibility.
Mobile and cell phone usage is possible but not recommended due to limited screen size and functionality.
